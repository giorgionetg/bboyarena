# BboyArena Roadmap

BboyArena is currently at v0.0.1 pre-game/prototype stage.

The priority is to keep the monorepo simple, avoid splitting into multiple repositories, and prepare legal and governance foundations early while the project is still small.

## Community Roadmap

- Keep the project visible and understandable.
- Collect feedback before major contribution work begins.
- Build a healthy path for credits, proposals, events and community experiments.

## Official Product Roadmap

- Focus first on offline/freeplay/training.
- Keep local/offline/non-commercial use accessible.
- Protect the official brand, ranked ecosystem, marketplace and certified server identity.

## Technical Roadmap

- Keep the monorepo simple.
- Avoid complex package structure until it is justified.
- Grow reusable technical code carefully around input, camera, state and browser game foundations.

## Federation Roadmap

- Treat federation as a design goal from the beginning, even if it is not implemented yet.
- Document server compatibility and certification expectations before public infrastructure grows.

## Commercial Roadmap

- Avoid pay-to-win mechanics.
- Explore sustainable paths around servers, events, creator tools, crew tools, cosmetics, merchandising, hosting, certification, sponsorship and private partnerships.

## Experimental Roadmap

- Use prototypes to validate movement, culture, controls, camera and session flow.
- Keep experiments clearly separated from official commitments until they are accepted into the roadmap.
