# Contributing to BboyArena

Draft policy. This document is intended to describe the current project intent and contribution rules. It is not legal advice and may be refined over time.

No substantial contribution will be accepted unless discussed and approved first.

This rule exists to:
- avoid contributors wasting time;
- protect the roadmap;
- avoid future disputes;
- keep the project founder-led and coherent.

## Contribution Flow

1. Proposal / Discussion
2. Approval to work
3. Pull Request
4. Review
5. Merge
6. Credits

## Contribution Categories

BboyArena may accept proposals for:
- code;
- bug fixes;
- gameplay proposals;
- moves/animation proposals;
- asset proposals;
- event/community posts;
- documentation;
- server/federation proposals.

## Rules

Contributors must have the right to submit their work.

Do not submit copyrighted, branded or third-party material without permission.

Do not submit AI-generated or third-party assets unless the rights are clear.

Feedback, ideas, issues, discussions and pull requests do not create automatic compensation, employment, partnership, equity, ownership or revenue share.

Important commercial collaborations require a separate written agreement.

Accepted contributors can receive credits.

Original cultural or animation contributions should be credited when accepted.

## CLA

Significant contributions require acceptance of [CLA.md](./CLA.md).
