# BboyArena Server Certification

Draft policy. This document is intended to describe the current project intent and contribution rules. It is not legal advice and may be refined over time.

This policy describes the intended server classes and certification model for future BboyArena online play.

## Server Classes

- BboyArena Official Server
- BboyArena Certified Server
- BboyArena Community Server
- Unverified Server
- Non-compatible Fork

## General Rules

Anyone may run local, private or community servers if technically possible.

Local and private servers do not automatically participate in official or global ranking.

Public non-commercial servers may exist but are not official by default.

Certified servers require approval.

Commercial or large-scale servers require approval and may require a monthly fee or private agreement.

Official global ranking is controlled by BboyArena.

Alternative servers may have their own ranking.

Official ranked and eSport tournaments require official or certified infrastructure.

Access to official auth, ranking or related APIs may require keys and approval.

Certification can be revoked for technical, cultural, anti-cheat, ranking, moderation, brand or commercial violations.

## Client Listing

The official client should be able to show official, certified, community and manual servers.

Unverified servers may be reachable manually via URL or configuration, but should be clearly separated from official and certified servers.
