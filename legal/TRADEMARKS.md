# BboyArena Trademark and Brand Policy

Draft policy. This document is intended to describe the current project intent and contribution rules. It is not legal advice and may be refined over time.

BboyArena name, logo, official marks, official characters, official stages and brand visuals are controlled project assets.

The software license does not grant rights to BboyArena trademarks, logos, official characters, official stages, music or branded assets.

## Compatibility Language

Users may say "compatible with BboyArena" only if the statement is truthful and not misleading.

Users may not say "official BboyArena" unless the project, server, event, asset, fork, client or service is operated by BboyArena or explicitly approved by BboyArena.

Suggested wording:
- Allowed: "Compatible with BboyArena protocol"
- Allowed if approved: "BboyArena Certified Server"
- Not allowed: "Official BboyArena Server" unless operated or explicitly approved by BboyArena.

Certified servers and certified events may use approved wording only.

## No Implied Endorsement

Forks, servers, events, mods, videos, communities and marketplaces must not imply endorsement, ownership, approval or official status unless that approval has been granted in writing.
