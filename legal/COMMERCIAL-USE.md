# Commercial Use Policy

Draft policy. This document is intended to describe the current project intent and contribution rules. It is not legal advice and may be refined over time.

## Free Uses

Allowed without prior approval:
- downloading the game;
- building it locally;
- playing offline;
- studying the code;
- learning web/game development;
- non-commercial modding;
- non-commercial community experimentation;
- small creator content with proper credits;
- using generic technical components for unrelated sectors.

## Requires Approval

Requires prior written approval:
- commercial use in the breaking/bboy/bgirl/hip-hop street dance segment;
- commercial tournaments;
- large creator channels using BboyArena as a regular product/content format;
- brand sponsorships;
- official BboyArena server certification;
- access to official ranked ecosystem;
- use of official marketplace infrastructure;
- use of BboyArena name/logo/brand assets;
- commercial server operation based on BboyArena identity.

## Not Allowed Without Agreement

- publishing a commercial BboyArena clone;
- using modified clients to access official ranked systems;
- misrepresenting a fork/server as official;
- using the BboyArena brand without approval;
- selling moves as gameplay advantage;
- pay-to-win mechanics.

## Pay-to-Win Rule

BboyArena must not become pay-to-win.

Monetization should be based on servers, events, creator tools, crew tools, marketplace/referral/affiliate, cosmetics/merchandising, hosting, certification, sponsorship and private partnerships.

## Creator Use

Small creators may publish gameplay, commentary, clips, tutorials and similar content with proper credit and links to official BboyArena pages.

Large commercial channels, regular monetized formats, sponsored content series, or channels using BboyArena as a recurring product/content format should request partnership or approval first.

## Marketplace

The target royalty for community marketplace assets should be low, around 3-5% where possible.

Larger infrastructure-heavy projects, brand partnerships, premium creator programs or official collaborations can use custom terms.

## Brands and Large Sponsors

Large brands, sponsors, agencies, international events or major creators require a private agreement.
