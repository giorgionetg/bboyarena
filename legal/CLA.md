# BboyArena Contributor License Agreement Draft

Draft policy. This document is intended to describe the current project intent and contribution rules. It is not legal advice and may be refined over time.

This is a lightweight contributor license agreement draft for significant contributions to BboyArena.

## Ownership

You keep ownership of your contribution.

By submitting a contribution that is accepted into BboyArena, you grant BboyArena a worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable license to use, reproduce, modify, distribute, sublicense and include the contribution in official BboyArena projects and services.

This includes future commercial services connected to BboyArena.

## No Automatic Commercial Rights

Submitting feedback, ideas, issues, pull requests, assets, animations, documentation or other work does not create automatic compensation, employment, partnership, equity, ownership or revenue share.

Paid work, commercial collaborations, branded work, premium assets, creator partnerships or revenue-share arrangements require a separate written agreement.

## Rights Confirmation

You confirm that:
- you have the right to contribute the work;
- the work does not knowingly violate third-party rights;
- the work does not include copyrighted, branded or restricted material without permission;
- any AI-generated or third-party material has clear rights for the proposed use.

## Community and Commercial Assets

Free community contributions can be used in the project and credited when accepted, but should not be resold as premium standalone assets by default.

Commercial assets, branded collaborations and paid creator work require separate agreements.
