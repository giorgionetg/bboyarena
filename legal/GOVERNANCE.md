# BboyArena Governance

Draft policy. This document is intended to describe the current project intent and contribution rules. It is not legal advice and may be refined over time.

BboyArena is community-driven and founder-led.

The community can propose, discuss, contribute, create mods, create events, create servers and suggest improvements.

Final decision authority over roadmap, brand, licensing, official ranked, marketplace, server certification, official events and monetization remains with the founder/project lead.

Maintainers may receive operational authority over specific areas.

## Possible Roles

Roles may include:
- core maintainer;
- gameplay maintainer;
- animation/move curator;
- cultural curator;
- server/federation maintainer;
- community moderator;
- marketplace reviewer;
- event partner;
- ambassador.

## Future Governance

The project can evolve toward a broader governance structure later.

Until explicitly changed, the canonical version is the official repository and official domains.

Canonical locations:
- `github.com/BboyArena/bboyarena`
- `bboyarena.org`
- future `play.bboyarena.org`
- future `api.bboyarena.org`
